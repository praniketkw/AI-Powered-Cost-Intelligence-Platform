# AI-Powered Cost Intelligence Platform

An enterprise-grade cost intelligence platform that correlates cloud spending with performance metrics, business context, and operational events to provide proactive, actionable cost insights. Think "DataDog for Cost Intelligence" with AI-powered root cause analysis.

## üéØ Project Overview

This platform solves the #1 problem in enterprise cloud management: **reactive cost discovery**. Instead of finding out about cost issues after they happen, our AI-powered system provides:

- **Proactive Cost Intelligence**: Predict cost issues before they impact budgets
- **Contextual Correlation**: Link cost spikes to deployments, incidents, and business events
- **Intelligent Alerting**: AI-filtered alerts that focus on actionable issues, not noise
- **Cross-Team Collaboration**: Bridge the gap between Finance, Engineering, and Business teams
- **Real-Time Root Cause Analysis**: Instantly understand why costs changed and what to do about it
